✦ Getting personal

About Me

I'm Aakshi — a passionate learner of web development and design, and a coffee addict who someday loves peace and someday is in a full-blown mood of chaos and parties. Somewhere between all the dreams, I'm still figuring out life, but I stay passionate about learning more and more beyond textbooks.

How I Discovered Myself

2024 — The Academic Chapter

Like many students, I had no clear idea what I truly wanted — only that I wanted to be successful, independent, and earn well. I was passionate, but practically so — shaped by others' expectations. I studied hard and performed great academically. Along the way, I found a small piece of myself: an interest in web development. I learned block coding, then HTML and CSS basics in 9th grade, but lost track when academics took over.

2025 — Class 11 & The Reset

I opted for PCM. Everyone said: cut off your social life, study hard, it'll all be worth it. I did — until September. In October, I chose to reconnect. I reached out to old friends, started socialising again. It felt light and happy. I met new people, had a great time, and found someone I could share everything with.

2026 — The Year That Shaped Me

January: lost my academic track. February: lost my grandfather unexpectedly. March: lost contact with someone important, and my friend group drifted as everyone changed career paths — including me. Each of these events, painful as they were, helped me discover who I really am. From April, I came back — to web development, to academics, to my friends — this time with balance.
